Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

We welcome both appointments and walk-ins! If you want to guarantee quick service, give us a call ahead at your convenience. Walk-ins are served on a first-come, first-served basis, typically with a short wait time depending on how busy we are.

What if I don't know what's wrong with my car?

No problem at all! That's what we're here for. Just describe what you're experiencing—strange sounds, warning lights, performance issues—and our technicians will run a full diagnostic to identify the problem. We'll explain what we find in plain language before we do any work.

Do you work on all car brands and models?

Yes, absolutely. We service all domestic and foreign vehicle makes and models—from Honda and Toyota to Dodge, Ford, Mercedes, BMW, and everything in between. Our technicians have the training and tools to handle any vehicle that comes through our doors.

How long do repairs usually take?

That depends on the repair. Simple services like oil changes take 15–30 minutes. More complex work like brake service or electrical diagnostics might take 1–3 hours. When you bring your car in, we'll give you an honest estimate of how long the work will take so you know what to expect.

Will you call me before starting work or adding costs?

100% yes. We never start work or exceed the quoted amount without your approval first. If we discover additional issues during the repair, we'll call and explain what we found before we proceed. You're always in control of what gets fixed.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, and financing options through Snap Finance. We also offer competitive labor rates and quality parts at fair prices—no hidden fees ever.

Can I get a second opinion on a quote from another shop?

Of course! We're happy to take a look and give you our honest opinion. Bring in the other shop's quote and we'll perform our own diagnostic at no charge so you can compare. We're confident you'll see we offer better value and more transparent communication.
